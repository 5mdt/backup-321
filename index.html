🔥 3-2-1 Backup Rule Cheatsheet

Don't fuck up.

3️⃣3 COPIES

  • 1 original
  • 2 backups

2️⃣2 TYPES OF MEDIA

  • Example: external drive + cloud
  • Don’t trust just one tech

1️⃣1 OFF-SITE

  • One backup stored away
  • Fire? Theft? Still got it

3️⃣3 COPIES

📄🖥️
Original File
💾📂
Backup #1
☁️🔐
Backup #2
Always keep 2 extra copies besides the original.

2️⃣2 TYPES OF MEDIA

💽🔌
External Drive
☁️📡
Cloud Backup
📼🧾
NAS or Tape
Use 2 different media: disk + cloud, USB + NAS, etc.

1️⃣1 OFF-SITE

🏠💻
At Home
☁️🔐
Cloud Backup
📦🏢
Office or Storage
Keep at least 1 copy far away:
Cloud, office, storage — just not home.
✅ Golden Rule:
If you don’t follow 3-2-1, you WILL lose your data.
Not "if" — when.
